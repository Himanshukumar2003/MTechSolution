ASPERA IP-58

  1. Home
  2. Aspera Redio

Overview

A PTT Over Cellular (POC) Radio in the form-factor of Mobile Radio that is IP-67 certified and is suitable for Fixed Location or for placing in Vehicles. Has all the features required in a POC radio including support for 4G network, GPS etc.

Key Feature

  • Cellular Radio (PoC/IP), No Limitation
  • In Distance Coverage (Nation Wide Call)
  • PoC/IP & PMR446 Options
  • Can work as PoC Only, PMR446 Only
  • PoC+PMR446 and as a Repeater
  • Private Call/Group Call
  • Dual PTT Key
  • Call Recording & Playback
  • Over the Air Programming
  • Supports 4G/3G/2G Networks
  • Programmable Keys
  • Loud & Clear Voice
  • Splash Proof

Main Specifications:

  • Analog Sensitivity: 0.2µV
  • Frequency Stability: ±2.5 PPM
  • Audio Output Power: 1.5W
  • Working Voltage: 3.7V
  • Battery Capacity: 4000mAh
  • Wide Working Temperature: -20°C to +60°C
  • Light Weight: 180g (including battery & antenna)
