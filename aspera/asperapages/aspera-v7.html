Overview

Among our earliest radios, this sleek & small VHF & 350MHz Radio is still counted among the better-looking radios. It offers longer talk time then then its sleek looks may suggest and have complete range of accessories to support user.

Key Feature

  • Emergency Alarm
  • 16 Channels
  • Charging Tray Included
  • Low Battery Prompt
  • Time Out Timer

Main Specifications:

  • Analog Sensitivity: 0.2µV
  • Frequency Stability: 2.5 PPM
  • Audio Output: 0.5 Watt
  • Working Voltage: 7.2V
  • Battery Capacity: 2000mAh
  • Wide Working Temperature: -200C to +500C
  • Light Weight: 190g (including battery & antenna)
