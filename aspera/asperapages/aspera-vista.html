Overview

Unique Patented design with a Translucent body that is easy to find in dark. It also has a hidden display that shows all the vitals of the radio in clear to read format. It provides unmatched performance along with an excellent built quality and strong fuselage.

Key Feature

  • Patented Design
  • Translucent Body that is Visible in Dark
  • Strong Fuselage, Excellent Built Quality
  • Hidden LED Display that will not break
  • 16 Channels
  • Low Battery Prompt
  • Time Out Timer
  • 21 Hrs Battery Backup
